Geometry Brawl

Geometry Brawl is a 2D real time PVP shooter. Every game is played in rounds, and the first player to reach 3 rounds won is the crowned the winner! To win a round players must maneuvre their character/shape around the arena and destroy the opposing player using an arsenal of different weapons which each have their unique traits and ability. Every kill gives characters experience points to upgrade and buy new weapons and characters in shop. Once you're ready, create an account and hop in game! Have fun!
